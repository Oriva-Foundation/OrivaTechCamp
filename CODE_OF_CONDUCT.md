# Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing an open, welcoming, and harassment-free environment for contributors, learners, and volunteers.

As contributors and maintainers of Oriva Tech Camp (OTC), we pledge to:

- Be respectful and kind in communication.
- Assume good intent and provide constructive feedback.
- Respect diverse perspectives, experiences, and identities.
- Avoid demeaning, exclusionary, or harassing behavior.

Unacceptable behavior includes — but is not limited to — violence, threats, harassment, discrimination, and the use of slurs.

Reporting
- If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the maintainers at contact@orivatechcamp.org. Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

Enforcement
- Maintainers will review reports and may issue warnings, remove content, or ban contributors in line with community standards.

Scope
- This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (GitHub, Discussions, Issues, PRs, and community channels) and events organized by OTC.

Thank you for helping us keep the community inclusive, safe, and constructive.
